WHO WE ARE

Started in 2020, Music Feedback is a Discord community built on the foundation of sharing industry experience and expertise. We help musicians further their careers and hobbies through critical and supportive advice while providing opportunities to demonstrate improvement and understanding.

Meet The Team

Jenn
Founder

Jenn is a classically trained musician with over 15 years of experience playing instruments and writing songs.

She has experience creating content for and managing social media, recruiting and contacting for A&R, working at a live venue, as well as researching and implementing PR campaigns for musical artists.

She offers insight and guidance for managing music releases, press and promotion, and critical listening for production, songwriting, and lyricism.

Dan
Admin

Dan first stepped foot on stage over 20 years ago. He studied vocal performance at the Musician’s Institute in Hollywood, CA and worked under late platinum songwriter/producer, Jim Huff (Joe Bonamassa - “Dirt In My Pocket”).

Though primarily a vocalist, he plays multiple instruments and is a seasoned guitarist. He is both the guitarist and second vocalist for the experimental rock project, AWES, and is the videographer for OPB Productions.

He is a bartender, enjoys camping, poker, and spending time with good friends. He derives most of his abilities from his awesome hair, which has been scientifically confirmed to power Manhattan for 7.5 years.

Troy #2

Troy #2 is a world-renowned electric kazooist. He has 53 years of experience and toured briefly with the legendary Dr. Erasmus Garfunkle. Born and raised in Pennsylvania, but now resides on his private alligator farm in Florida, collecting guitars as a hobby.

He also holds an honorary bachelor's degree in Hydrology from FU.
