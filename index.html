Community Events

Artist of the Week

Artist of the Week is a biweekly event celebrating a server member who has recently finished or released an original song or cover. The member is assigned the Artist of the Week role for 2 weeks and their music is broadcasted to the server.
Read more

Music Feedback Listening

Music Feedback Listening is an ooportunity for members to receive live feedback on current works in progress. Members have a 20 minute window to submit their songs and join the voice channel. The community then provides live critical feedback. Music Feedback Listening is frequently scheduled on Thursday nights.
Read more

Short Song Challenge

Short Song Challenge is a timed event where members must create a song based on a prompt within 1 hour. This challenge is open to songwriters, lyricists, and producers, but the submission must be presented as a playable audio file. Short Song Challenge is announced on the day of the event.
Read more

MF Fest 2021 & 2022

MF Fest was a summer event last held in 2022 to showcase the many special talents of our songwriters, instrumentalists, and performers. During the event, members recorded mini sets and longer headlining performances on The Only Stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

The feedback channels through #feedback-access offer a one-for-one system that guarantees quality feedback on audio, lyrics, and vocals. Members are required to first give objective and critical feedback before they can ask for advice on their own song. Points are tracked through the implementation of <MFR and <MFS bot commands.

If feedback is not received after 48 hours, members may tag @Moderators to receive help.

Artist of the Week is held on a biweekly schedule. Every other Sunday, eligible members may submit their song to be voted upon for the popular vote. Another week will follow for follow up questions from members and submissions will reopen. Music Feedback Listening and Short Song Challenge will occur when our Event Hosts are available.

To keep up with our events, make sure to assign yourself the roles to find out when they're happening!

There are three main channels for posting your original music: #intro-music, #finished-music, and #finished-lyrics.

New members may post in #intro-music for the first 8 hours after joining. After that, #finished-music can be used until the Stagehands role is reached. All works-in-progress may be posted in #works-in-progress.

The Music Feedback server has an entire channel dedicated to the collaboration process through #collaboration-access. Members may send out real-time alerts for specific roles they need filled, like Singers, Rappers, or Instrumentalists.

The #service-portal channel also allows for the advertisement of paid personal services, like mixing and mastering.

Ranks are based on activity in the server. Deranking happens after 1 month of inactivity.

Who We Are

Started in 2020, Music Feedback is a Discord community built on the foundation of sharing industry experience and expertise. We help musicians further their careers and hobbies through critical and supportive advice while providing opportunities to demonstrate improvement and understanding.
